THE CREDENTIAL

A certification employers can trust.

The BullForce Certified Technician (BCT) credential isn’t a participation trophy. Every tech who carries it has cleared the same bar:

✓Completed all four certification courses — online modules and supervised field days
✓Scored 75% or better on every written exam
✓Passed a hands-on field assessment — equipment, technique, and job-site conduct
✓Demonstrated PPE discipline and completed a job hazard analysis, OSHA-aligned
